Most of the energy used today are obtained from the fossil fuels. It is predicted that we will run out of fossil fuels in this century. Oil can last up to 50 years, natural gas up to 53 years, and coal up to 114 years. Thus switching to renewable source energy is the need of the moment. Nowadays people use smart devices like Wi-Fi lights and fans, but when smart bulbs are compared to regular LED bulbs,
the cost outweighs the reason to switch to a smart bulb. Moreover individual smart device consumes extra power (even when turned off) to communicate with other devices on the network.

Thus we need to make an appealing product which will make us switch.


The Objective


Our main objective is to create a smart device consuming low energy as well as low cost and to combine it with renewable energy sources to make it more appealing. We want to popularize adoption of both renewable energy sources and smart home solutions with our product.

Power Saving Mode


This mode was designed by our team so that in case of a prolonged power outage the hub can be self-sufficient in running the necessary appliances to make life easier. By turning on this mode the house goes into a state of low energy consumption mode.

It saves power by:

Turning off high voltage appliances like air conditioner to reduce energy usage.
Adjust light use, like turning off the decoration lights in garden or the fountain Turns off idle devices like coffee maker, micro oven when not in use.
Dims lights so that it consumes less power and the battery lasts longer.
Uses machine learning to reduce power consumption.

The SCH app

• The amount of energy harvested from solar panels.
• The amount of capital saved by upgrading to smart technology.

• The amount of electrical power saved.
